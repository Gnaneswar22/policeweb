SRIKAKULAM POLICE

Srikakulam district was first carved out in 1950 from Visakhapatnam district. Again in May, 1979, the district was bifurcated on account of formation of Vizianagaram district. The Headquarters of Srikakulam District Police started functioning from the year 1974 after bifurcating from Vizag North.

Topography is Vizianagaram dist. flanks in the South and West Odisha state bounds on the North and the Bay of Bengal on the East.

Area of the district is 5,837 Sq.k.m and 193 kms of length of coast line. Length of National Highway-16 is 174 k.m and population is 27,03,114 as per census 2011. Total no of villages are 1865 .

Out of 46 Police stations, 43 L&O PSs, Traffic, CCS and Disha PS. 12 Circles and 3 Sub-divisions exist in Srikakulam district. 3 Marine Police Stations are Kalingapatnam, Bhavanapadu and Ekkuvuru.
