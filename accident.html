Accident Analysis

Understanding accident trends is crucial for improving road safety. Here are some insights:

Recent Trends

  • Increase in accidents during monsoon season
  • Majority of accidents involve two-wheelers
  • High accident rates near school zones

Preventive Measures

We are implementing measures such as:

  • Increased patrolling in high-accident areas
  • Public awareness campaigns
  • Collaboration with local organizations for safety workshops
