Women's Corner

Welcome to the Women's Corner of Srikakulam Police Department. We are committed to ensuring the safety and security of women in our region. Our team is available 24/7 to assist women in distress, and we offer a range of services specifically tailored to support women's safety and well-being.

If you or someone you know is facing harassment, domestic violence, or any other crime, please do not hesitate to reach out to us. We are here to help.

Helpline Numbers

Women's Helpline: 1091

Domestic Violence Helpline: 181

Child Helpline: 1098

Police Control Room: 100

Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930

How We Can Help

  • Emergency Support: We respond promptly to any emergency reported by women.
  • Counseling Services: Our team includes professional counselors who can guide and assist women in crisis.
  • Legal Assistance: We provide legal aid to women facing harassment or domestic violence.
  • Protection Orders: Assistance in securing protection orders for women at risk of harm.
  • Safe Shelter: Immediate arrangements for safe shelter for women in need.
