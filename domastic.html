Domestic Violence Support

Understanding Domestic Violence

Domestic violence includes physical, emotional, psychological, and financial abuse. It can affect anyone, regardless of age, gender, or socioeconomic status.

Available Resources

  • Emergency Helpline: Call 100 for immediate assistance.
  • Women's Helpline: Call 181 for support and resources specific to women.
  • Counseling Services: Access professional counseling through local NGOs and support groups.
  • Legal Assistance: Contact legal aid services for guidance on your rights and options.

How to Report Domestic Violence

If you or someone you know is facing domestic violence:

  1. Call the emergency helpline: 100
  2. Visit the nearest police station to file a report.
  3. Fill out the online reporting form on our official website.
